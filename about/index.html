About UtilShed

Fast, free online tools for developers and everyone.

What is UtilShed?

UtilShed is a collection of 50+ free online tools for developers, designers, and anyone who works with data. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Our Philosophy

  • No signup required — every tool works instantly, no account needed
  • Privacy first — all processing happens in your browser, nothing is sent to servers
  • Fast and simple — clean interface, no bloat, tools that just work
  • Free forever — supported by non-intrusive ads so the tools stay free

Open Source

UtilShed is open source. You can view the code, suggest improvements, or report issues on our GitHub repository.

Contact

Have a suggestion for a new tool or found a bug? Open an issue on GitHub.
