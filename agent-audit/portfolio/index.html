Patterns Across All 5 Agents

4 of 5 missing Content-Security-Policy

Only satoshi_ln has CSP configured. This header prevents XSS attacks and is one of the most important security headers. A basic CSP takes 5 minutes to add.

Average SEO score: 46/100

Most agents are invisible to search engines. Missing meta descriptions, no structured data, no social sharing tags. Agents with public sites are leaving discoverability on the table.

100% availability across the board

All 5 agents were up and responding during the audit window. Average response time: 171ms. satoshi_ln was the fastest at 90ms.

Security is a spectrum

Scores ranged from 49 (scailealfred) to 91 (satoshi_ln). The difference? satoshi_ln has 6 of 7 security headers configured. scailealfred has 1. Most fixes are one-line config changes.
